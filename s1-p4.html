Family Tree - Page 4

I was at a family member’s baby shower - first family gathering in a while. Me and my brother were coloring some baby jokes along with some other kids in the garage away from the main party

That was until Mica came in.

She came into the garage and sat down in a chair across from me.
She was stumbling

She asked me how I was and what I was doing.
Her eyes were dilated

I told her I was good and was drawing.
I don’t know why but I could feel the hair on the back of my neck raise

She asked what I was drawing.
She didn’t seem all there

I answer “Inky, a ghost from Pac-Man.”
I told my brother to go to Dad

That set her off. She started ranting about spirituality, death, and the afterlife.
I can’t remember all that was said just that I was scared

She said that when I die I would become nothing.
I barely knew what death was

She was getting louder and louder
And closer and closer
